Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions about digital security? We've got answers. Check out our FAQ section to learn more about how DESCAM protects you from scams and fraud.

How does DESCAM detect scams? +
DESCAM uses advanced AI algorithms to analyze patterns, behaviors, and digital footprints to identify potential scams. Our system continuously learns from new threats to stay ahead of scammers.
Is my personal data safe with DESCAM? +
Absolutely. We use military-grade encryption and never store sensitive personal data. Our systems are designed with privacy as a top priority.
What types of scams can DESCAM protect against? +
We protect against phishing, identity theft, financial fraud, investment scams, romance scams, tech support scams, and emerging threats like deepfake fraud.
How quickly does DESCAM respond to new threats? +
Our threat intelligence team works 24/7 to identify and respond to new scams. Most new threats are added to our detection systems within hours of discovery.
Can DESCAM help if I've already been scammed? +
Yes, our recovery specialists can guide you through the process of reporting the scam, securing your accounts, and potentially recovering lost funds.
Is DESCAM suitable for both individuals and businesses? +
Absolutely. We offer tailored solutions for individuals, small businesses, and large enterprises with scalable protection for all needs.
How does the deepfake detection work? +
Our system analyzes digital media for subtle artifacts and inconsistencies that indicate manipulation, using advanced neural networks trained on thousands of examples.
What makes DESCAM different from other security services? +
DESCAM focuses specifically on scam prevention rather than general cybersecurity. Our specialized approach means deeper expertise and more effective protection against fraud.
